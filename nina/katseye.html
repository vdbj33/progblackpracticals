✧ KATSEYE ✧

Link to Katseye's page

Katseye is a global girl group formed through a joint venture by record labels Hybe Entertainment and Geffen Records via the survival show Dream Academy.

The group is made up of 6 members from a diverse range of backgrounds:

  • Megan
  • Yoonchae
  • Daniela
  • Lara
  • Sophia
  • Manon

Katseye have released several hit songs since their debut in 2024, not limited to but including:

Debut Released 28 June 2024
Touch Released 5 August 2024
Gnarly Released 30 April 2025
Gabriela Released 20 June 2025
